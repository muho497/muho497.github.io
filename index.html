🎯 My Philosophy

I strive to learn about artificial intelligence both in-depth and from a broad perspective.

In this process, I also explore other subjects that interest me and integrate some of them into the field of AI. My primary areas of reading span a wide range, starting from popular science to neuroscience, robotics, physics, biology, philosophy, chemistry, materials science, astronomy, mathematics, art, literature, ecology, architecture, and economics.

In short, I am deeply fascinated by the working principles and mechanisms of anything that has a design.

I love nature and believe it enhances my creativity. I enjoy asking questions like, "How can we do this better?" and "How can we optimize this further?" I prefer creating over consuming and embrace the idea of using consumption as a means of production.

My Vision:
The idea of building the future together excites me, and I take great pleasure in the innovations it will bring. Let's design the future together as humanity!
